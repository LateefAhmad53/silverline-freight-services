1. Service Overview

Silverline Freight Delivery Services provides logistics and delivery solutions designed to ensure safe, secure, and timely transportation of packages. Our process includes several mandatory stages required to meet international and local shipping standards.

2. Delivery Process Requirements

To ensure successful delivery, all shipments must pass through the following stages:

  1. Security and Safety Processing
  2. Clearance Procedures
  3. Regulatory Agency Processing
  4. Final Handling

Each stage is essential to comply with shipping regulations and to guarantee the safe handling of all packages.

3. Processing Charges

Charges are applied at each stage of the delivery process. These charges are:

  • Mandatory for all shipments
  • Temporary in nature
  • Required to facilitate compliance with security, customs, and regulatory requirements

Failure to complete any required payment may result in delays or inability to complete delivery.

4. Refund Policy on Processing Charges

All charges incurred during the processing stages are fully refundable upon successful delivery.

These fees are not permanent service costs. They are temporary deposits used to ensure that all regulatory and safety procedures are properly completed.

Upon successful delivery:

  • The full amount of all processing charges will be refunded
  • Refunds will be made through the original payment method, or
  • Returned directly to the recipient by the courier at the point of delivery

5. Non-Refundable Fees

The original shipping fee paid by the sender is non-refundable. This fee covers the actual transportation and logistics service provided and remains valid regardless of delivery outcome.

6. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate delivery and contact information
  • Complying with all payment requirements during the shipment process
  • Ensuring that shipped items comply with applicable laws and regulations

7. Delays and Exceptions

  • Customs or regulatory authorities

  • Unforeseen circumstances beyond our control
